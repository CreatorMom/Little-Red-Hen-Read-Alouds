Welcome to Little Read Hen Read Alouds!

Basic Info

Here at Little Read Hen Read Alouds ♥ we read the classic fairy tales, Aesop's Fables and more!
We read books such as Little Red Riding Hood, The Little Red Hen,
The Three Little Pigs, and even darker tales by Brother's Grimm such as Hensel and Gretel!
I will be reading these stories in many different versions!
If that sounds like fun then come join me!!!

Why Read the Classics?
  • They provide wholesome lessons
  • Exciting Plots
  • They never get old!

Wholesome

Wholesome is defined as conducive to or protmoting moral well-being.
Words and stories are powerful ways to communicate healthy messages to children.
Messages that can influence for a lifetime!

Exciting Plots

The plots in these stories generally start with some sort of conflict
that leads the characters on some sort of journey that then leads them to some sort of resolution

Transcend Time

The lessons within these classics do not expire! Their concepts are
and will always remain relatable
